Eidolon

Comics

Inspired by Greek mythology and a 1837 French short novel titled La Vénus d’Ille (Ille’s Vénus), by Prosper Mérimée, Eidolon is a story about two young lovers who elope into a ruined city to marry at the feet of the statue of the goddess of love. However, events take a worrying turn: the statue comes to life and goes after the groom, while the bride is taken by an ill-willed conspirator. Helped by a savant and an enchantress, they must fight to survive, reunite and love again.

The synopsis is done. The script is ongoing revisions. We are looking for partners to make this project a reality.

Interactive table

We also design new kind of devices. We are currently looking for funds to produce interactive tables, with applications pertaining to gaming, education, arts and design.

Functions

  • Input and output communication channels using sound, image, and touch. Main channel: interactive table surface.
  • Projectors would allow usage of walls and projection screens to multiply the display surfaces.
  • Able to detect the position of what is laid on it. If useful, it might also detect the position of users and follow their gestures.
  • Tables would connect to each other and constitute a network of interactive hot points.
  • Exchanging productions is at the heart of the project, and a peer-to-peer marketplace is integral to it.

The interactive space includes not only the table, but also the volume around it.

Why?

The project revolves around three main objectives

  • Interactivity: users will have a larger array of inputs and outputs to play, work and create, including voice, pointers, controllers touch, sound and images. Since it’s meant for transmedia applications, it requires a lot of capabilities.
  • Conviviality: nowadays, most digital equipment is meant for a single user or a few users facing a screen. Our users will face each other in an interactive space. Linking tables together will create a network of interactive hot points. Several tables in the same place would ideally be able to conjugate their spaces.
  • Creativity: we want to turn automation and digital tools into production means to enhance creativity, not to create instead of the user. Bringing several users in a high interactive space will also foster creativity.
